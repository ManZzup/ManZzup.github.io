IAESTE Sri Lanka

About what we do

We are the Sri Lankan Local committee of IAESTE, the world’s biggest trainee exchange organization for students of engineering, science, agriculture and forestry. Sri Lanka has been a member of IAESTE since 2000 and has quickly become one of the dream internship destinations for so many students from all corners of the world.

Beyond the actual internship placement, IAESTE provides accommodation in the destination country and offers cultural programs in most countries. Local committee of Sri Lanka ,which runs by bunch of university students, take care of the incoming interns and do the basic paperwork with the immense help of National Committee.

If you would like to apply for an IAESTE internship in Sri Lanka you should contact the local/national committee in your city/country. Visit the International IAESTE website to find their contact information.

If you have already gone through that step and know you are coming to Sri Lanka, well, AyuBowan (means ‘Welcome’). You can browse these pages to get a preview of Sri Lanka. Also, please contact us as soon as possible so we can greet you when you reach here.

Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, A country of rich values and culture

Introduction

Sri Lanka, island country in the northern Indian Ocean off the southeast coast of the Indian subcontinent in South Asia. Sri Lanka has maritime borders with India to the northwest and the Maldives to the southwest. Sri Lanka has a documented history that spans over 3,000 years, but there are theories to suggest that Sri Lanka had pre-historic human settlements dating back to at least 125,000 years. Its geographic location and deep harbors made it of great strategic importance from the time of the ancient Silk Road through to World War II. Sri Lanka is a republic and a unitary state governed by a presidential system. The capital, Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte, is a suburb of the largest city, Colombo. It is also an important producer of tea, coffee, gemstones, coconuts, rubber, and the native cinnamon, the island contains tropical forests and diverse landscapes with a high amount of biodiversity.

For a small island, Sri Lanka has many nicknames: Teardrop of India, Pearl of the Orient etc. This colorful collection reveals its richness and beauty, and the intensity of the affection it evokes in its visitors. Consequently this small island state contains an exotic mixture of languages, cultures and religions.

Employer Resources

How to join with IAESTE, benefits of our program and more

Established in 1948, the IAESTE Association runs/offers an international exchange programme with partner agencies in more than 80 countries. With over 60 years experience of exchanging over 4000 trainees a year, IAESTE is the tool to help for internationaliseing and growing your business.

As an employer, IAESTE gives you:

  • Access to a pool of more than 10,000 Science, Engineering and Applied Arts undergraduates and recent graduates, in over 1,000 universities from over 80 countries world-wide.
  • Highly skilled workers at low cost
  • An opportunity to learn about the culture and technological expertise of other countries
  • A chance to view your enterprise from a different perspective and develop present and future business partners within a global context.

And, because IAESTE is a reciprocal program, providing a placement for an international trainee allows a student from your country the chance to go abroad to gain valuable work experience.

Your Benefits

Tailor Made and Expert

  • IAESTE is an employer-led programme, meaning students are selected according to your requirements, which can stipulate criteria such as: subject area; specialization; level of study; technical experience, nationality and duration.
  • IAESTE trainees are ideally placed to carry out short-term projects and to help develop new business opportunities overseas.

Skilled and High-calibre

  • Students are hand-picked (carefully selected) through a stringent, 3-tier selection process from a pool of over 1000 universities worldwide
  • Hard working, high caliber and highly motivated trainees, with appropriate technical knowledge and experience
  • Trainees with excellent communication skills (English is mandatory for the participation in the exchange programme)
  • Previous IAESTE trainees have worked on award winning projects, been credited in published academic research and been offered employment upon completion of their placement both overseas and in their home countries

International

  • IAESTE students bring an international dimension to the workplace as well as fresh ideas and different, often innovative perspectives.
  • If your company is looking to expand into new international markets, IAESTE is a great way to build contacts and cultural links, as well as ambassadors for your company

Admin-free

IAESTE Sri Lanka will provide expertise (and the global IAESTE network, support) in navigating necessary visa/work authorization, facilitating accommodation and organizing social and cultural activities for the trainees. IAESTE Sri Lanka will be able to entirely facilitate these on your behalf, leaving you hassle-free to get the most out of the placement.

Reciprocal

  • IAESTE is a reciprocal programme, so your offer of a placement for a foreign student guarantees a placement overseas for a local undergraduate student, directly building the local skill base
  • All students gain great experience to put on their CVs and return with increased confidence and renewed enthusiasm for their field
